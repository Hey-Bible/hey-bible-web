In accordance with the App Store and Google Play Store policies, Working Dev's Hero LLC provides a way to delete your Hey Bible account and all associated data.

To delete your account, please follow the steps below.

  1. Log into Hey Bible
  2. Navigate to the Account page
  3. Tap the "Remove Account" button
  4. Confirm the deletion by clicking "Remove"

When you remove your account, your name, email, verses, favorites, and images will be deleted immediately.
