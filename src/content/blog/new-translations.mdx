We're excited to announce a major expansion of Hey Bible's translation library! You can now read, search, study, and create verse art in seven Bible translations:

- **Christian Standard Bible (CSB)** 🆕
- **English Standard Version (ESV)**
- **King James Version (KJV)**
- **New American Standard Bible (NASB)** 🆕
- **New King James Version (NKJV)** 🆕
- **New Living Translation (NLT)** 🆕
- **World English Bible (WEB)** 🆕

## Powered by API.Bible

These translations are made available through our partnership with [API.Bible](https://api.bible), the industry-standard Scripture API. This integration gives us access to high-quality, licensed text from major publishers including Thomas Nelson, Holman Bible Publishers, and Crossway.

## Citations in Verse Art

As part of our licensing agreements, every verse art image you create now includes a proper citation. For example, NKJV verses now carry a compact inline notice right below the verse:

> John 3:16 • NKJV  
> © 1982 Thomas Nelson. All rights reserved.

This clean, unobtrusive format ensures our community can share beautiful Scripture images while honoring the publishers who make these translations available.
